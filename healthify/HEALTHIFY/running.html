Running

Running is a highly effective cardiovascular exercise that helps in improving heart health, burning calories, and building endurance. It engages multiple muscle groups and can be performed anywhere.

Benefits of Running

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Burns calories and aids in weight management
  • Enhances lung capacity and endurance
  • Strengthens leg muscles
  • Boosts mental health and reduces stress

How to Perform

1. Warm up with light jogging or stretching.

2. Start at a comfortable pace and maintain proper posture.

3. Breathe rhythmically and stay hydrated.

4. Gradually increase speed and distance over time.

5. Cool down with walking and stretching exercises.
